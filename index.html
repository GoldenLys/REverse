Introduction

Welcome to REverse, a game created by Purple Wizard.
The main goal of this game is to fight enemies and progress through the story to gain EQUIPMENT and EXPERIENCE.
You'll discover & explore a new world filled with many adventures !
But be aware: there are powerful monsters that you will need to fight wisely; it's up to you to find your own way to do so! ðŸ˜‰

Username

Enter your desired username for your character (3-16 alphanumerical characters).
Write "Neo" to generate a random name.

Avatar

You can also choose your avatar; it will be shown during any fight.

Class

You will need to select a class, which gives you a different starting bonus per class.

WARRIOR

Warriors form the frontline of any fight. They deal the most damage.
(+5% Damage)

PALADIN

Paladins fight for justice. They protect the weak with their strong shield.
(+5% Armor)

NINJA

Ninjas are elite fighters, planning and plotting everything before they fight.
They can learn the basics faster than anyone.
(+5% Bonus Experience)
